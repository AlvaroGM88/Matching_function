A BILL
To direct the Secretary of Transportation to make grants
for the operation of a clearinghouse to collect, conduct,
and fund research on the influences of highly automated
vehicles on land use, urban design, transportation, real
estate, and municipal budgets, and for other purposes.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives 
of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Preparing Localities
for an Autonomous and Connected Environment Act’’ or
the ‘‘PLACE Act’’.
SEC. 2. GRANTS FOR THE OPERATION OF A NATIONAL
HIGHLY AUTOMATED VEHICLE CLEARING-

‘highly automated vehicle’ means a motor vehicle

(including steering, accelerating and decelerating, and reacting to external stimulus) without
human intervention.

OF HIGHER EDUCATION.—

meaning given the term in section 101 of the Higher
Education Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 1001).

term ‘secondary influence’ means the influence on land use,
